**Link a term when:**
- It appears in prose/context sections
- It is a key concept that benefits from expansion

**Do NOT link:**
- Terms inside LaTeX math environments (`$...$` or `$$...$$`)
- The term being defined in its own definition
- Every occurrence‚Äîlink only the **first meaningful occurrence**
