---
title: "Commutative ring axiom"
description: "Axiom requiring multiplication in a ring to be commutative."
---

The **commutative ring axiom** asserts that multiplication satisfies
\[
ab=ba\qquad\text{for all }a,b\in R.
\]

A {{</* knowl id="commutative-ring" text="commutative ring" */>}} is a {{</* knowl id="ring" text="ring" */>}} satisfying this axiom (and usually also the {{</* knowl id="unital-ring" text="unital" */>}} axiom). Commutativity is essential for the theory of {{</* knowl id="prime-ideal" text="prime ideals" */>}} and for geometric results such as the Nullstellensatz.
