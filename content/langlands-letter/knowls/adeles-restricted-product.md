---
title: "Adeles and Restricted Products"
description: "The adele ring $\\mathbb{A}_F=\\prod_v' F_v$ used for automorphic forms"
---

Let $F$ be a number field with completions $F_v$.

Given compact open subrings $\mathcal O_v\subset F_v$ at almost all finite $v$, the **restricted product**
$
\prod_v' F_v=\{(x_v): x_v\in \mathcal O_v \text{ for almost all finite }v\}.
$

The **adele ring** is $\mathbb{A}_F:=\prod_v' F_v$ (with $\mathcal O_v$ the local integer rings at finite $v$).

**Key property:** $F$ embeds diagonally into $\mathbb{A}_F$, and automorphic forms live on $G(F)\backslash G(\mathbb{A}_F)$ (see {{< knowl id="automorphic-form-hecke-eigen" section="langlands-letter/knowls" text="automorphic forms" >}}).
