---
title: "Semidirect Product"
description: "A group $\\Gamma\\ltimes H$ built from an action of $\\Gamma$ on $H$"
---

Let $\Gamma$ and $H$ be groups and let $\Gamma$ act on $H$ by automorphisms ($\gamma:h\mapsto {}^\gamma h$).

The **semidirect product** $\Gamma\ltimes H$ is the set $\Gamma\times H$ with multiplication
$
(\gamma,h)(\gamma',h')=(\gamma\gamma',\,h\cdot {}^\gamma h').
$

**In the letter:** the {{< knowl id="l-group-satake-parameter" section="langlands-letter/knowls" text="$L$-group" >}} is a semidirect product of a Galois group with the {{< knowl id="langlands-dual-group" section="langlands-letter/knowls" text="dual group" >}}.
