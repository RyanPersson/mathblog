---
title: "Dual Lattice"
description: "The $\\mathbb{Z}$-dual $L^\\vee=\\mathrm{Hom}(L,\\mathbb{Z})$ and its role in dual root data"
---

A **lattice** is a free abelian group $L\cong \mathbb{Z}^r$.

Its **dual lattice** is
$
L^\vee := \mathrm{Hom}_\mathbb{Z}(L,\mathbb{Z}).
$

A pairing $\langle\cdot,\cdot\rangle: L\times L^\vee\to \mathbb{Z}$ is given by evaluation: $\langle \lambda,\mu\rangle=\mu(\lambda)$.

**In the letter:** "conjugate lattice" $cL$ is a dual lattice used to build the {{< knowl id="langlands-dual-group" section="langlands-letter/knowls" text="dual root datum" >}} and the Satake parameter space.
